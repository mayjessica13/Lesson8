Meet the Players

Brian Posehn - comedian/ actor/ writer/ metalhead, known for writing and appearing on Mr. Show with Bob and David and more recently collaborating with Bob Odenkirk and David Cross on the book "Hollywood Said No!: Orphaned Film Scripts, Bastard Scenes, and Abandoned Darlings from the Creators of Mr. Show" published in 2013. He was also a fixture on The Sarah Silverman Program. A true Renaissance man, Posehn has appeared in a huge number of sitcoms and movies, written and co-written graphic novels and comic books, released comedy albums, and tours constantly as a stand-up. Somehow he fits regular recordings of Nerd Poker into all of this.

Blaine Capatch - comedian/ writer/ musician, has appeared on Mr. Show, the Comedians of Comedy, the Eleventh Hour, and hosted the second season of Comedy Central’s Beat the Geeks. He has written on the Mind of Mencia, Blue Collar TV, MADtv, Web Soup, and @Midnight. He also plays guitar for The Tulsa Skull Swingers and regularly appears at Lucha Va Voom. His incredibly quick-witted banter, "Brought to you by" spots, and obscure television and pop culture references keep the Nerd Poker listeners on their toes.

Ken Daly - actor/ writer/ producer, was a child actor who appeared in various tv shows such as Simon & Simon and Quincy M.E., as well as a McDonald’s commercial for Shamrock Shakes, and movies that include The Karate Kid and The Usual Suspects. He appeared in several episodes of Mr.Show and an episode of The Sarah Silverman Program.

Gerry Duggan - writer, friend and writing partner of Brian Posehn (they co-wrote The Last Christmas). He was adept at creating D&D characters that would immediately annoy Brian and make his character want to kill them. Gerry eventually had to bow out of Nerd Poker, mainly because he seems to be a writer on numerous Marvel comics (Hulk, Batman: Arkham Manor, Deadpool, Chewbacca, Uncanny Avengers, and Nova) and Sunset Overdrive on the Xbox One. It’s no surprise that Gerry had no time for regular games of Dungeons and Dragons. He is missed.

Sarah Guzzardo - writer and something of a mystery. She used to run the website Avoidthisjob.com. Sarah has very little online presence, possibly because she’s not an actor/entertainer and is more of a normal person. She remains the only past or present "cast member" of Nerd Poker who is a woman. She is missed.

Scott Robison (Mr. Sark) - gamer/ host/ dungeon master, reviewed games on G4TV’s X-Play and then moved to Machinima Respawn. He now has a presence on Youtube (youtube.com/themrsark) and Twitch (twitch.tv/themrsark). Both loved and reviled by the group for his horrendous impersonations, love of Michael Bay movies, and for being "a shitty DM", he left the show shortly before his first kid was born. Like Gerry and Sarah, he is missed.

Nerd Poker with Brian Posehn and Friends is an production.
