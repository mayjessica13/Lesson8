Episode Index - The First Adventure

Episode #1 - Let the Game Begin!

Brian Posehn engages in a weekly game of Dungeons & Dragons with his funny friends Gerry Duggan, Blaine Capatch, Ken Daly, Sarah Guzzardo, and Dungeon Master Scott Robison (Mr. Sark). This week they explain the game of D&D, introduce their characters, and embark on their adventure aboard a pleasure cruise.

Episode #2 - Roll For Your Fate

The group finds themselves stowed away on a pleasure cruise with swashbuckling hot chicks as their captors. The dimmest of the bunch, Amarth Amon, volunteers to hurt someone and the group weighs whether they need a more stealth approach. Blackee Green, a half elf warlock, does reconnaissance with the dungeon master’s helpful world building. After gaining intel Mildred Maxton, Bartho Shett, Ell Ryan and the rest of the gang weapon up.

Episode #3 - In Search of Gear

More of the mysterious ship is unraveled as the gang moves forward in their adventure. Amarth Amon, Blackee Green, Mildred Maxton, Bartho Shett, Ell Ryan are led by the dungeon master (Mr. Sark) as they search high and low for their gear. Will they take the stealthy route or go out guns blazing?

Episode #4 - Let’s Get Ready To Rumble

It’s time for the gang to rush, strike, and belch their way through a couple of guards as they continue their journey to find their gear! Amarth Amon is first to deliver a devastating strike, Blackee Green sits back to decide what his next move will be, Mildred Maxton wonders if they should take a guard hostage, Bartho Shett thinks cleaving rocks, Ell Ryan unleashes the wrath of winter, and dungeon master (Mr. Sark) guides them along the way.

Episode #5 - Shipwrecked

A large rift has left the gang stranded on the sinking ship. Amarth Amon and Mildred Maxton must lash together the remaining wreckage to sail off into safety. Before they depart, Blackee Green, Bartho Shett, and Ell Ryan explore the ship for food and supplies. What does Mr. Sark have in store for them next? Will they survive? Can the spirit bear swim?

Episode #6 - Swim Toward The Light

The sun has set and the gang has no choice other than to swim in the darkness toward the specs of light up ahead. Ell Ryan summons his spirit bear to gain an advantage while Amarth Amon, Blackee Green, and Bartho Shett paddle their way toward the light using the sail that Mildred Maxton managed to rig. Mr. Sark will be their guide to what lies ahead in this pitch-dark night.

Episode #7 - Follow the Whispers

The gang has no choice but to follow the whispers up ahead to find out what happened to Ell Ryan. Mildred Maxton takes action and dives underwater in search of Ell Ryan, Blackee Green tries to make out the whispered conversations to gather clues, Amarth Amon stealthily swims towards the voices, and Bartho Shett is tired. All the while, Mr. Sark helps them advance by whispering as well.

Episode #8 - The Survivors

With Ell Ryan’s disappearance, the gang decides to follow the survivors who have mistaken them for collectors. Ermine the survivor (Mr. Sark) guides Amarth Amon, Mildred Maxton, Blackee Green, and Bartho Shett to their underwater sanctuary.

Episode #9 - Going In Recons Blazing

The gang decides to regroup back at the survivors’ underwater home to figure out a plan to infiltrate the collectors’ base. They learn about the connection with the Sea and Ell Ryan’s death as they talk to the Elder and decide to infiltrate the collectors’ base in the morning. Survivors Tras and Carn (Mr. Sark) join Amarth Amon, Mildred Maxton, Blackee Green, and Bartho Shett on their mission to find out what lies inside the collector’s base.

Episode #10 - On The Catwalk

Amarth Amon, Blackee Green, Mildred Maxton, and Bartho Shett have successfully made it inside the collector’s base with the help of Tras and Carn (Mr. Sark). They’ve been careful not to get spotted until they reach the catwalk. Now they must roll to see if they can reach a running collector before he warns others of their presence. Will luck be on their side or will they roll botches?
